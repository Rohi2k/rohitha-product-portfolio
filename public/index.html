FEATURED CASE STUDY

Agent R - AI-assisted Reel Production Workflow

A product case study showing how a manual reel creation process can become a structured, human-reviewed, AI-assisted workflow.

Problem

Manual reel production involves repeated effort across research, scripting, scene planning, prompt writing, asset generation, timing, and review. Agent R explores how this workflow can be structured into a repeatable AI-assisted product process.

Product Work Completed

Created product proposal, Proof of Value, MVP plan, workflow map, metrics, review checkpoints, and early draft reel validation.

Current Status

Proposal, POV, and MVP plan completed. Early workflow feasibility tested using two draft reel outputs.

Workflow
01 Raw Story

Input story, target audience, and content intent.

02 Research

Clarify context, facts, tone, and story angle.

03 Script

Create hook, narration, scene flow, and CTA.

04 Scenes

Break script into visual scenes and asset prompts.

05 Timing

Prepare dialogue timing and edit-ready structure.

06 Human Review

Check accuracy, tone, visuals, and final creative direction.

07 Measure

Track time saved, rework, quality, and output readiness.
